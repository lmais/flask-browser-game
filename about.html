About


This website is a choose-your-own-adventure style game which allows kids ages 7 - 9 to help our protagonist, Adalita the Alpaca, find her way through the mountains to reconnect with her family. Along the way she will face many challenges, and you must answer math problems to help her achieve her goal. The map on the right side of the game screen will show you how far you have left to go. Adalita only has ten days to meet up with her herd before they migrate to a new pasture, will she make it in time?
